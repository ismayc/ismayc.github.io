YOUR GOAL

🎯
Draft the team in each group you think will win and reach the conference finals.
After 4 rounds, each player will have exactly one team per group (4 teams total).
ADVANCING TO O/U FINALS
The 2 players with the most teams reaching the conference finals advance to the O/U Finals.

Tie-breaker goes to the higher regular season seed.

This means Phil only needs 1 team to make the conference finals to guarantee his spot. Everyone else needs 2 to lock it up.

DRAFT ORDER

PICK TRACKING

Picks carry across rounds

⚠️
Important: If you use a pick in an earlier round (by being forced to pick when everyone above you passed), that pick is gone for later rounds.
EXAMPLE
In the examples, Jake was forced to use his #2 pick in Round 1 (chain of passes) and voluntarily used his #3 pick in Round 2 to lock in a team.

When Round 3 arrives and Jake is at position #2, he cannot use it because it's already spent. He passes, Phil jumps ahead and grabs a better team, and Jake ends up last.
💡 Strategy tip: Passing early lets you save your pick for a round where you have stronger preferences. But you risk getting stuck with a less desirable team if others pass to you. Check out the "Lost Pick" example on the previous page to see this in action.
